Health and Safety at Work Act (HASWA) & Hair Professionals

Core Duties

What HASWA Requires

Employers must:

  • Provide safe equipment and systems of work.
  • Assess and control risks; provide training & supervision.
  • Maintain a safe and healthy salon environment.

Employees must:

  • Take reasonable care of themselves and clients.
  • Follow training and use equipment safely.
  • Report hazards and cooperate with procedures.
Umbrella

Key Regulations Under HASWA

  • MHSWR 1999: Risk assessment & training.
  • COSHH 2002: Safe use of hair chemicals.
  • RIDDOR 2013: Reporting work-related illness/injury.
  • PUWER 1998: Safe salon equipment use.
  • PPE 1992/2022: Gloves, aprons, masks.
  • Workplace Regs 1992: Welfare, ventilation, cleanliness.
  • Manual Handling 1992: Safe lifting & posture.
  • Electricity at Work 1989: Safe use of dryers/clippers.
  • Fire Safety Order 2005: Alarms, evacuation, training.
  • First Aid 1981: Adequate first aid provision.
